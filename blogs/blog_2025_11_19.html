> UTOPIA


A utopia refers to an imagined place or state of things in which everything is perfect.
Tragically, it's the complete opposite description for the current times, but the road to a utopia sure shows what my mind lives for.
Utopia represents what someone does looking for the actual utopia, those actions that lead to the ultimate outcome where effort will pay off, not necesarilly with benefits but with the feeling that you are not stuck, the story is progressing.

If you don't perceive everyone (including yourself) as characters, and years as chapters, then what even keeps you going? Create a plot that'll give meaning to your life.

Lately, lots of things haven't been going as I expected. When that happens it isn't usually that big of a deal, but because of it this time I'm not really sure what my future plans are. It's truly a nightmare.


Uncertainty is such a punishing aspect of this situation. Even when my thoughts get debunked and the situation starts to clear up, there's a blur that won't go away.
